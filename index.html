About News Publications Experience Services Misc

I am a research scientist and tech lead at Hillbot Inc. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in ECE at UC San Diego in 2025, advised by Prof. Hao Su. Before that, I received my B.E. degree in computer science and technology from Tongji University in 2020. My research interests are in Computer Graphics, Computer Vision and Embodied AI.
